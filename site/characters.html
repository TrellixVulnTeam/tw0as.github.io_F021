CHARACTERS

There are 46 playable characters in Genshin Impact.
Characters can primarily be obtained by using Intertwined Fate or Acquaint Fate (purchased with Primogems though also available through Events, Quests or exchanged monthly for Masterless Stardust or Masterless Starglitter at Paimon's Bargains) to perform Wishes. Other ways to obtain characters include: archon quests (Amber, Kaeya and Lisa are obtained during the Prologue Chapter), Paimon's bargains, permanent events (Barbara and Xiangling), and limited time events.
